Cleaning Scholarships

​The Cleaning Scholarship is a generous working-award for families in need of assistance and who are willing to clean the school’s campus.

Scholarship Benefits and Requirements
  • There are four scholarships available and will pay for $70 off of tuition for one child for any class (TTH or MWF) every month in exchange for 4 hours of indoor cleaning or yard maintenance.
  • Scholarships will be awarded twice a school year for each fall and spring semester by drawing names from a pool of entries submitted by the due date emailed by the president.
  • Cleaning responsibilities are clearly designated on the inside of the kitchen door whereupon it is the duty of the recipient to monitor which tasks have been completed and to supplement cleaning in areas not completed by the cleaner the week before.
  • It does not relieve the member of their obligation for fundraising, volunteer hours or registration fees.
  • Scholarship recipient must remain in good standing to keep the scholarship (ex. attend all meetings/workdays, yard days, support job. etc).  
  • Standing will be re-evaluated mid-year in January or as needed.
  • The Soquel PENS Scholarship Committee reserves the right to revoke the scholarship at any time should any requirement not be met.
