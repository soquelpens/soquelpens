A Community Treasure Since 1949

Soquel Parent Education Nursery School (Soquel PENS) is a parent participation preschool serving families in the Santa Cruz County foothills. Our non-profit parent cooperative has been an integral part of this community since 1949, offering quality education to both child and parent.

"I wish more families knew that Soquel PENS was such a fabulous place for both preschoolers and parents to learn and grow into incredible members of the community." — Mary H., Soquel PENS alumna parent

Our Program Philosophy

"We have had two children go through the co-op. I can't imagine a stronger, better foundation for our children — or for us as parents." — Soquel PENS parent

At Soquel PENS, children learn about themselves and the world around them through play. By participating in a stimulating learning environment with other children their own age, students develop skills and build confidence — socially, emotionally, cognitively and physically.

Parents actively involved in their child's learning are better able to understand and meet their child's needs. They observe credentialed teachers and practice new skills in a supportive environment. Monthly evening meetings offer in-depth parent education from teachers and guest speakers.

Children will…

  • Discover joy through age-appropriate play
  • Develop social skills and unique strengths
  • Build kindergarten readiness
  • Benefit from high adult-to-child ratio
  • Feel a sense of belonging and acceptance

Parents will…

  • Gain confidence in child development
  • Practice positive discipline skills
  • Actively participate in education
  • Benefit from a supportive community
  • Enjoy lower tuition through involvement

Together you will…

  • Make lifelong friends
  • Share joy, comfort and pride
  • Build a true sense of community
  • Experience two-generation growth
  • Carry these friendships for years
"The cooperative organization provides preschool experiences within the financial means of most families — and a community that enriches every aspect of life." — Soquel PENS, founded 1949

Our Two Programs

Choose the schedule that fits your family. Both programs run September through June, following the Santa Cruz school calendar.

Mon · Wed · Fri

Three-Day Program

Our most popular option, giving children rhythm and consistency while forming deep friendships across the full week. Up to 15–16 children plus siblings.

Ages 2½–5  ·  9:00 AM – 11:30 PM

Tue · Thu

Two-Day Program

A wonderful introduction to co-op preschool. Ideal for younger children and families new to the cooperative model. Up to 14–16 children plus siblings.

Ages 2½–5  ·  9:00 AM – 12:30 PM

How to Enroll

  • 1

    Contact our Membership Coordinator

    Email soquelpensmembership@gmail.com to express interest. Spaces are filled on a first-come, first-served basis.

  • 2

    Pay the enrollment deposit

    Submit a $100 non-refundable enrollment fee via PayPal, cash, or check (made out to Soquel PENS, child's name in memo).

  • 3

    Complete registration paperwork

    Pick up your registration packet at school. Include immunization records, a TB test result (within 4 years), and all completed forms.
