REGISTRATION

Information and Enrollment Procedures
Once you have been offered a space at Soquel PENS by the Membership Coordinator, you will need to complete the registration process to enroll your child. Please find the procedures for the enrollment process below.

​If you are interested in enrolling your child, but have not been contacted the Membership Coordinator, please email our Membership Coordinator at soquelpensmembership@gmail and we will contact you with further steps.
Registration - Current school year
To register during the current school year, follow the steps below:
​
​STEP 1: Click the link below, complete the Online Registration Form and press SUBMIT
ONLINE REGISTRATION FORM
STEP 2: Download the Requirements Contract for the year of your enrollment below and submit the form with your packet​. Pay $100 Non-Refundable Registration Fee (Please pay via PayPal, cash or check made out to Soquel PENS. Please write your "Child's Name and class in the memo line. (Ex. Enrollment Deposit, Jane Jones, MWF).
​
REQUIREMENTS CONTRACT
​STEP 3:​ Pick up the full registration packet at school. Fill out packet CAREFULLY. Instructions will be included to help you navigate all the steps.
          
STEP 4:​ Provide a copy of a current, negative TB test result that was taken within four years that does not expire until the end of your enrollment year.

STEP 5:​ Provide a copy of your child's immunization record or a medical exemption.

STEP 6:​ Fall or Spring WASCAE Registration fee of $50 (Please attach to registration packet, cash or check made out to WASCAE with the title, "Fall or Spring REG Fee/Soquel PENS" in the memo line.

STEP 7:​ Refundable Attendance Deposit Fee of $50 (Please pay via PayPal, cash or check made out to Soquel PENS. Please write your "Child's Name/Class MWF or Tue/Thu  in the memo line and attach it with your registration packet. (Ex. Attendance Deposit/Jane Jones/MWF.)

STEP 8:​ Return fully completed paperwork to our school for review with two attached checks (Adult School Registration Fee & Attendance Deposit). Incomplete forms will be returned for re-submission. You will be handed the Fingerprint Live Scan Form at this time.

​STEP 9:​ You're ready to start school! Once teachers receive word  of your fingerprint clearance (which can take 1 to 3 weeks)  you will be notified and be able to work in the classroom.
