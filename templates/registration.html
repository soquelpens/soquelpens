Once you have been offered a space by the Membership Coordinator, complete the steps below to enroll your child. If you have not yet been contacted, email our Membership Coordinator at soquelpensmembership@gmail.com and we'll reach out with next steps.
Current School Year

Registering for 2025-2026

1

Complete the Online Registration Form and press Submit.

Online Registration Form
2

Download and submit the Requirements Contract with your packet. Pay the $100 Non-Refundable Registration Fee via PayPal, cash, or check made out to Soquel PENS — include your child's name and class in the memo (e.g. Enrollment Deposit, Jane Jones, MWF).

Requirements Contract 2026–2027
3

Pick up the full registration packet at school. Fill it out carefully — instructions are included to guide you through each step.

4

Provide a copy of a current, negative TB test result taken within four years that does not expire before the end of your enrollment year.

5

Provide a copy of your child's immunization record or a medical exemption.

6

Pay the $50 WASCAE Registration Fee (Fall or Spring). Attach to registration packet — check made out to WASCAE with "Fall or Spring REG Fee/Soquel PENS" in the memo line.

7

Pay the $50 Refundable Attendance Deposit via PayPal, cash, or check made out to Soquel PENS — include your child's name and class in the memo (e.g. Attendance Deposit/Jane Jones/MWF).

8

Return your fully completed paperwork to school with both checks attached (Adult School Registration Fee & Attendance Deposit). Incomplete forms will be returned. You will receive the Fingerprint Live Scan Form at this time.

9

🎉 You're ready to start school! Once teachers receive word of your fingerprint clearance (1–3 weeks), you'll be notified and able to work in the classroom.

Upcoming School Year

Registering for 2026-2027

1

Complete the Online Registration Form and press Submit.

Online Registration Form
2

Pay the $100 Non-Refundable Registration Fee via PayPal, cash, or check made out to Soquel PENS — include your child's name and class in the memo (e.g. Enrollment Deposit, Jane Jones, MWF).

3

Download and submit the Requirements Contract with your packet.

Requirements Contract 2026–2027
4

Mail in the contract. You may drop it off in person until May 30th, after which the school closes for summer.

5

During summer — the full registration packet will be mailed to your home. If you don't receive one, pick it up at:
WASCAE Santa Cruz Center, 319 La Fonda Ave, Santa Cruz, CA 95062

6

Provide a copy of a current, negative TB test result taken within four years that does not expire before the end of your enrollment year.

7

Provide a copy of your child's immunization record or a medical exemption.

8

Pay the $50 WASCAE Registration Fee (Fall). Attach to packet — check made out to WASCAE with "Fall REG Fee/Soquel PENS" in the memo line.

9

Pay the $50 Refundable Attendance Deposit via PayPal, cash, or check made out to Soquel PENS — include your child's name and class in the memo (e.g. Attendance Deposit/Jane Jones/MWF PM).

10

During summer, you will be notified of August paperwork dates. Bring your fully completed packet with both checks (Adult School Registration Fee & Attendance Deposit). Incomplete forms will be returned. You'll receive the Fingerprint Live Scan Form at this time.

11

🎉 You're ready to start school! Once teachers receive word of your fingerprint clearance (1–3 weeks), you'll be notified and able to work in the classroom.
